We’re excited to announce that Cohesity Cyber Event Response Team (CERT) services are now available to enterprises with NetBackup deployments. This expansion builds on the success of broadening our CERT service for Cohesity DataProtect users through strategic partnerships with leading incident response (IR) vendors, announced earlier this year . CERT delivers a powerful blend of technology and cyber response expertise to help you recover faster in the wake of cyberattacks.
