With Pure Storage’s customer conference, Pure Accelerate 2025 , happening June 17-19 in Las Vegas, Cohesity is excited to announce new data protection capabilities that are designed to enhance cyber resilience, simplify operations, and accelerate innovation for Pure Storage customers. New: Data protection of Pure Storage Protection Groups Cohesity now supports backup of Pure Storage Protection Groups for Pure Storage FlashArray block storage to deliver powerful end-to-end data protection and cyber resilience for modern enterprises.
